Data Localisation

All data for Indian businesses is stored within Indian data centre boundaries in compliance with applicable data localisation requirements. We use RBI-approved data centres for financial data processing.

KYB (Know Your Business)

Our verification processes align with RBI KYB guidelines and MCA21 standards. We cross-reference data with official government registries including MCA, GST Network, and income tax databases to ensure authenticity.

Audit & Reporting

We maintain comprehensive audit trails for all data access and processing activities. Regular compliance audits are conducted by independent third-party auditors. Reports are available to enterprise customers on request.
