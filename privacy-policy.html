Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16.12.2025

Dotko.in (“Dotko”, “we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal and business information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and disclosed when you use the Dotko’s web based version of B2B Pre Trade Intelligence platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign up on Dotko, we collect the information that is directly filled by you:

  • Account Information - Business Name, Entity Type
  • GSTIN, PAN (masked and encrypted)
  • Contact details (name, mobile number, email, WhatsApp number)
  • Address and business details
  • Transaction-related information (invoice number, date, amount, credit period, invoice or ledger copy)
  • Uploaded documents (invoice copies, communication proof)
  • Usage Information - How you interact and use our platform, includes pages visited, features used and search queries

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information only to:

  • Provide, maintain and improve our B2B verification services
  • Notify buyers and suppliers about reported transactions
  • Analyze payment patterns and predict behaviours
  • Send you technical notices, updates and administrative messages
  • Generate Trust Scores and ratings based on defined logic
  • Improve platform safety, security, and user experience
  • Detect, Investigate and prevent fradulent activites
  • Comply with legal obligations under MSME and other applicable laws

📌 We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Sharing & Visibility Rules

  • Uploaded documents are never made public
  • Buyer ratings become public only after due process
  • Information may be shared with:
    • The counter-party involved in a reported transaction
    • Law enforcement or authorities if legally required
    • With government databases to verify business information
    • With third party vendors who perform services like cloud hosting and analytics on our behalf

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored on secure servers
  • Sensitive data is encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Access is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain information:

  • As long as your account is active
  • As required for dispute resolution, audit, and legal compliance
  • Business verification data may be retained for compliance purposes as required by applicable laws

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access and update your information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request account deletion (subject to legal retention)

Requests can be sent to: trust@dotko.in

7. Children’s Privacy

Dotko is intended for business users only. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and by updating the Last Updated Date. We encourage you to check the privacy policy periodically.

9. Contact

For questions regarding the data practices we implement, you can contact us at:

📧 trust@dotko.in
