Account Deletion

Request Account Deletion

We're sorry to see you go. If you wish to delete your DOTKO account and all associated data, please read the information below carefully before proceeding.

Important: This Action is Permanent

Deleting your account will permanently remove:

  • Your profile and business information
  • All uploaded documents (GST, PAN, licenses, etc.)
  • All reports you have filed
  • All communication history
  • Your trust score and verification status
  • All app settings and preferences

Note: Reports filed against your business by others may be retained for legal and record-keeping purposes as required by law (up to 7 years).

Before You Delete Your Account

Consider these alternatives:

  • Temporarily disable notifications in app settings
  • Update your privacy settings to limit data sharing
  • Contact our support team to resolve any issues

How to Delete Your Account

1

In-App Deletion

Open the DOTKO app → Go to Profile → Settings → Scroll to bottom → Tap "Delete Account" → Confirm deletion

2

Email Request

Send an email to support@dotko.in with subject line "Account Deletion Request" including your registered email and reason for deletion (optional)

3

Verification

We'll verify your identity and process your request within 48-72 hours. You'll receive a confirmation email once completed.

Data Retention Policy

After account deletion:

  • Personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Backup data is purged within 90 days
  • Transaction records may be retained for 7 years as required by Indian business law
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely
